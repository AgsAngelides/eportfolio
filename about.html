Hello, I'm Ags!

My birth name is actually Agne Angelides, however, I have been known as Ags for the past 10 years and Agne is now something only used by my mother and grandma or those who want to offend me. To help new people I meet to remember my name, I often say it's like 'Bacon and Ags'. I moved to the UK 10 years ago from a small Baltic country Lithuania. We Lithuanians are hardworking and can be proud people. We also often say what we think, which I found often gets me into interesting situations. I currently reside in the naval heart of England - Portsmouth, which Dickens found remarkable for the amount mud and sailors once.

My background

In 2017 I have graduated from the university of Southampton where I have achieved BSc in Physics (Hons). Since then I have been working as a Project Mananger/Business analyst on various Information Technology projects for a small family like IT business.
